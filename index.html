Cruise Race


You don't need to rush while on vacation, but granny got lost on the Lisbon layover and ran late to continue her cruise adventure... The ship is about to leave, hurry up - if you can - and help granny get on the cruise.


Controls


Use RIGHT and LEFT arrows to move granny - and yes... she walks extremely slowly (Be patient or catch some HELPERS). The SPACE button puts you back in the initial position without restarting the game.



Objects


  • OBSTACLES:How can anchors fall from the sky? Anyway... These elements are heavy and would immediately kill granny. Be extremely careful and avoid them!


  • HELPERS: A sip of martini for the vacation mood! It will speed ​​up granny so she can get on the cruise ship right on time


  • KEY: Granny is very distracted, so you must catch her boarding pass. Without it, you will not be able to enter the cruise.
